Welcome to the LSF Guides! Here you'll find plenty of information of love and sex in the furry fandom boiled down to simple guides. These are all free, and all open-source, so you can contribute on your own as long as you have a GitHub account!

Available Guides

Safer Sex

Covering the topics of consent, protection, and contraception in a clear and topical manner to help you make healthy and informed choices.

Relationships

Covering the topics of relationship status within the furry fandom, polyamory, online and long distance relationships, communication, and sex and intimacy.

Gender

Covering the topics of gender and sex, trans* identity, the intersection with sexuality and furry, and social interaction.
